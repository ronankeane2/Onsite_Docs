Calendars¶

The powerful calendar functionality is central to the application.

  • The Company-wide Calendar

  • Dashboard Specific Calendars

  • Calendars are Fully Interactive

The Company-wide Calendar¶

The ‘company-wide’ calendar is accessed from the calendar icon at the top of the screen, and can be filtered by controls up at the top.

  • Filter by Task

  • Filter by Staff

  • Filter by Parent Category

Dashboard Specific Calendars¶

There are dedicated calendars for sales, installation, servicing and production (factory). These are accessible from the relevant dashboard.

Calendars are Fully Interactive¶

  • View by week or by month.

  • ‘Bars’ on calendars display relevant information from the task and order.

  • Hover over the entry to get more information like the address and contact name.

  • Click through the entry to view full task and/or associated order information.

  • Calendar entries update (e.g. are removed) according to updating of the task status.
